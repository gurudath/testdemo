The Vedas for the Present Pulse

Flying in the zenith of digital revolution and swaying in the highest wave of modernization, the humans undoubtedly host the privilege to enjoy and explore the different forms of creation to achieve his means of comfort living.

But his journey towards achievement of material comforts and mortal joy is getting intense to the extent of exploiting nature and disturbing its intricate balance .His life styles are getting far away from nature and thus loosing the subtle web of harmony with it. The repercussion of this is evident in the form of undesired calamities and various dreadful diseases.

It is high time that we resort to the righteous ways of living and leading life as reveled by the ancient texts of The VEDAS - The eternal treasure of India.

The VEDA and AYURVEDA:

The esteemed Indian System of Philosophy foresees every aspect of Life with highest reverence and grateful attitude. Thus accordingly renders the pursuits of Human Life to be the 'Dharma', 'Artha', 'Kama' and 'Moksha'. Each pursuit is bound with spiritual reasoning and mindful responsibilities, which are immortally recorded in the divine texts of the 'Vedas'.

Hence, The Vedas are the manifestation of sanctity to host the utmost code of conduct of every human being towards nature ."Ayurveda " is the branch of Atharva Veda which can be considered as the "Manual of wellness and healing"

Vision :

To unravel the aspects of Ayurveda to lead a life close to nature and living in harmony with it.

To create awareness for maintenance of health and wellness by following the regimens of 'Ayurvedic Way of Life'.

To provide holistic approach and a comprehensive treatment for each and every health issue or concern.

Mission:

To conduct Health awareness program with an intension to impart the Ayurveda code of Conduct for Healthy Living.

Posting various articles in the direction to create health awareness in the society.

To provide expert consultation and treatment for every infirmity.
